Privacy Policy - Comet Collections Extension

Last Updated: September 21, 2025

We do NOT collect any personal data

This extension stores all data locally in your browser and never transmits information to external servers.

What We Store Locally

  • Collection names you create
  • Page URLs and titles you manually add
  • Theme preferences
  • Extension settings

Permissions Used

  • activeTab: To get current page info when you add it
  • tabs: To open pages from collections
  • storage: To save collections locally
  • identity: Only for Chrome Web Store license verification
